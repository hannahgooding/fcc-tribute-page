Timeline

  • 1974 Hello Kitty is created by Ikuko Shimizu, a designer for Sanrio.
  • 1975 The feline character appears on her first piece of merchandise - a vinyl coin purse.
  • 1978 Sanrio begins selling merchandise in Europe and goes on to sell over a million Hello Kitty digital watches over the next two years.
  • 1983 Hello Kitty becomes a UNICEF Special Friend of Children.
  • 1987 Her first animated series is released entitled "Hello Kitty's Furry Tale Theater".
  • 1990 The theme park Sanrio Puroland opens in Tokyo, Japan where fans can meet Hello Kitty and her friends for the first time.
  • 1999 Hello Kitty's boyfriend, Dear Daniel, is introduced.
  • 2007 Hello Kitty makes her first appearance in the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade.
  • 2012 Hello Kitty runs for president in the USA under the Friendship Party.
  • 2014 The world descends into chaos when a statment is released by Sanrio stating that Hello Kitty is not a cat, but in fact a British school girl.
